Welcome to the LYCAN

We are group 1-4 in the Junior Core. LYCAN stands for "Learn Your Classmates and Names". We realized that it was difficult to learn more about our fellow classmates within our section as well as other sections and we know we weren't the only ones encountering this issue. By studying names, photos, fun facts, and other qualities members of the Core have, our website allows you to win friends and influence people.

Our website allows users to look at flashcards of students across the various sections of the 2022-2023 IS Junior Core. Our Library page lists all of the students currently in our system. It also gives the user a way to add other students we may not have yet! Once you're ready to start studying, you can head over to our Flashcards page and see our flashcards of students. This page includes a filter section so you can narrow the number of flashcards you see. When you click on the image of a student, the card will flip and allow you to see the students information. Finally if you'd like to give us any feedback or message us about any questions, go to our Contact page and send us an email!

We hope this website helps our classmates get to know each other better and make our Junior Core a stronger and more unified group!
